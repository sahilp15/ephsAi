## Known limitations (from the dataset)

- The PDF does not provide course numbers for the catalog, so course_number is intentionally omitted.
- Enrollment capacity, live seat counts, and teacher/period assignments are not present in the PDF and must not be fabricated.
- Some courses are cross-listed in multiple departments and may contain slightly different prerequisite wording; all appearances and conflicts are preserved.
- Symbols such as *, **, •, TC, and @ on pathway index pages are preserved as raw markers because their exact meanings are not fully defined in the extracted pathway pages.
- Graduation requirements are not presented as a single complete credit-audit table in this guide. The application must show a counselor-verification warning for any requirement not explicitly encoded.
