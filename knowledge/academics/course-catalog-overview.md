## College-level options

EPHS offers **40+ college-level courses**, and enrollment in them has doubled over
the past four years. Students are encouraged to take at least one college-level
course before graduating. College-level options include AP courses, honors courses,
PSEO, and courses with **articulated college credit**.

## How to choose courses

Course selection for 2026–27 happens in **Infinite Campus** (see the Course Selection
Process topic for dates). Counselors are available to help — book through Acuity and
choose the **Course Selection Support** appointment option:
https://app.acuityscheduling.com/schedule/1c840dc8

If you want details on a specific course (prerequisites, credits, grade level), check
the Course Selection Hub above — I may not have every individual course description
verified here.
