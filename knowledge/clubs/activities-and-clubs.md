# Clubs, Activities & Athletics

Eden Prairie High School (Home of the **Eagles**) offers a wide range of clubs,
activities, and athletics. Getting involved is one of the best ways to meet people and
explore interests.

## How to get involved

1. Check the official EPHS activities/athletics pages for the current list of clubs,
   sports, and meeting times.
2. Most clubs welcome new members anytime — show up to a meeting.
3. Reach out to a club's staff advisor with questions.

## Want to start a club?

Starting a club usually requires a staff advisor and approval from the activities
office. Contact the main office (952-975-8000) to be pointed to the activities office
for the current process.

I don't have the full, current roster of EPHS clubs and activities verified here, so
for the official list (and athletics), please check the EPHS site:
https://www.edenpr.org/high-school

If you tell me what you're interested in (sports, arts, STEM, service, culture), I can
suggest where to look — but always confirm the specific club exists on the official
page.
