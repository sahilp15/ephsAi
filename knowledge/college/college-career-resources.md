# College & Career Planning

EPHS supports students planning for college and careers through counseling, course
Pathways, and college-level course options.

## Ways to explore college & career

- **Pathways & Capstones** — focused course sequences in a career area, with chances
  to earn college credit (see the Pathways and Capstone Courses topic).
- **AP / Honors / PSEO** — college-level coursework and credit (see the AP, Honors,
  and PSEO topic).
- **Your counselor** — for applications, transcripts, testing, financial aid
  questions, and mapping courses to your goals.

## Plan early with your counselor

Course and college decisions build on each other, so start early. Book a counselor
appointment via Acuity: https://app.acuityscheduling.com/schedule/1c840dc8

For specific college/career resources (deadlines, transcript requests, financial aid),
confirm the current details with your counselor and the counseling team page:
https://my.edenpr.org/high-school/counseling-team-at-ephs
