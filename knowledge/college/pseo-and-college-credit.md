# PSEO & Earning College Credit

EPHS students have several ways to earn college credit while in high school.

## PSEO (Postsecondary Enrollment Options)

PSEO lets eligible students take college courses — often at no cost — for both high
school and college credit. Eligibility, deadlines, and how credits apply to your
graduation plan are important to confirm with your counselor, since they affect your
schedule and requirements.

## AP courses

AP exams (given each May) can earn college credit and/or advanced placement at many
colleges, depending on your score and the college's policy. See the AP, Honors, and
PSEO topic for the list of AP courses.

## Articulated college credit

Many career/technical courses offer **articulated college credit** earned through the
high school course. Look for this in the course descriptions on the Course Selection
Hub: https://www.edenpr.org/ephs-courses
