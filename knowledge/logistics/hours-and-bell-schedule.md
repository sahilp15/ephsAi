# School Hours & Bell Schedule

- **Student day:** 8:35 a.m. – 3:20 p.m., Monday–Friday
- **Office hours:** 7:30 a.m. – 4:00 p.m., Monday–Friday

The school day follows a set bell schedule, and there are special schedules on some
days (late starts, early releases, assemblies, etc.).

I don't have the exact period-by-period bell times verified here, so please check the
official EPHS page for the current daily bell schedule and any special-day schedules:
https://www.edenpr.org/high-school

For the school-year calendar (no-school days, conferences, breaks), see the Calendar
& Important Dates topic.
