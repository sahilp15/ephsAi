# Parking, Buses & Transportation

## Buses & transportation

For bus routes, eligibility, and transportation questions, contact the
**Transportation department at 952-975-7500**, or see the district transportation
information on the EPHS/Eden Prairie Schools site.

## Student parking

Student parking at EPHS typically requires a permit, and details (cost, how to apply,
rules) are on the official parking page. I don't have the current permit details
verified here, so please check:

**Parking information:** https://my.edenpr.org/parking

If you can't find what you need, the main office (952-975-8000) can point you to the
right person.
