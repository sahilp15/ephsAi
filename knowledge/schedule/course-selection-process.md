# Course Selection Process & Timeline (2026–27)

Students select courses for the 2026–27 school year in **Infinite Campus**.

## Key dates (2026–27 selection)

- **January 29** — Grade-level meetings & Course Selection Rush
- **February 2** — Course Selection opens
- **February 2, 6–8 p.m.** — Course Selection Open House at EPHS (families welcome;
  general session plus breakout sessions)
- **February 13** — Course Selection ends

> Dates are from the 2026–27 Course Guide. Always confirm current dates on the Course
> Selection Hub, since they can change year to year.

## How it works

1. Attend your grade-level meeting / Course Selection Rush and review the Course Guide.
2. Explore courses, Pathways, and college-level options on the Course Selection Hub:
   https://www.edenpr.org/ephs-courses
3. Enter your selections in **Infinite Campus** during the open window (Feb 2–13).
4. Meet with your counselor if you have questions or want help planning.

## Counselor appointments

Counselors are available throughout course selection. Book an appointment through
**Acuity** and choose the **Course Selection Support** option:
https://app.acuityscheduling.com/schedule/1c840dc8
