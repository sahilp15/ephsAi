# Schedule Changes

Your **school counselor** handles schedule changes. The best first step is always to
talk with them about what you want to change and why.

## How to start

1. **Book a counselor appointment** through Acuity:
   https://app.acuityscheduling.com/schedule/1c840dc8
2. Or email your assigned counselor directly (see the Counseling Team topic to find
   yours by last name).

## Good to know

- Schedule changes are usually only possible during specific windows, and not every
  request can be granted — it depends on space, prerequisites, and your graduation
  requirements.
- Dropping a course after a deadline may affect your transcript, so ask your counselor
  before making changes.

I don't have the exact schedule-change deadlines verified here — your counselor and
the official counseling page have the current rules and dates:
https://my.edenpr.org/high-school/counseling-team-at-ephs
