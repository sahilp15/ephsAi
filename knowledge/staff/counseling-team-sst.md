# Counseling Team & Student Support Teams (SST)

EPHS organizes student support into **Student Support Teams (SST)**. Each SST includes
an associate principal, an administrative dean, counselors, and a social worker who
work together to support students. **You are assigned to an SST and counselor based on
the first letters of your last name.**

## What counselors help with

Course planning, schedule changes, graduation progress, college and career planning,
and connecting students to support resources.
