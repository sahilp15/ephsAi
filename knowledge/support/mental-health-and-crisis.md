# Mental Health & Crisis Support

If you're struggling, you are not alone, and asking for help is the right move.
Reaching out to a real person is always best.

## Who can help at EPHS

- **Your school counselor** — for stress, personal struggles, or just to talk
  (book via Acuity: https://app.acuityscheduling.com/schedule/1c840dc8).
- **Your Student Support Team (SST)** — includes a social worker and counselors.
- **Any trusted adult** — a teacher, coach, or staff member you feel safe with.

## Crisis resources (available 24/7)

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** — call or text **988** anytime; free and
  confidential.
- **Crisis Text Line** — text **HOME** to **741741**.
- If someone is in immediate danger, call **911**.

## More support

Eden Prairie Schools has mental-health support information here:
https://my.edenpr.org/mental-health

Please talk to a real person — a counselor or trusted adult is there for you. An AI
assistant can't provide counseling or crisis support.
